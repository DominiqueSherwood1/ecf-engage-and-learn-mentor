Support for early career teachers

Mentor prototype

Iteration 1

For this iteration we mainly tested if the half term and weekly structure, the task list as a way of tracking ECT and mentor progress and a few potential features that mentors might find useful in their meetings with the ECT.

Some design variations (pages not tested):

  • Variation - list
  • Variation - accordian

Iteration 2

For this iteration, we separated out the ECT and metor progress and simplified progress tracking for mentors, including only materials for the self study and meetings).

Iteration 3

For this iteration, we tested the sign in process which also gave users the opportunity to add a username. We also tested a prompt to and guidance content piece and visualised different ideas for tracking, to help confirm what it was that mentors needed.

Iteration 4

For this iteration, we removed progress tracking and concentrated on getting the labelling for the different materials right. We also iterated the homepage, to see what information mentors might need on there and moved the username functionality to the homepage.
